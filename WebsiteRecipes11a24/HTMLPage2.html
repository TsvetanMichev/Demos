Quesadilla

  • PREP TIME - 5 mins mins
  • COOK TIME - 7 mins hours
  • TOTAL TIME - 12 mins hours
  • SERVING - 1 per person

Ingredients:

INGREDIENT AMOUNT
Large flour tortillas by choice
Grated cheese 1+1/2 Cups
Olive oil 1 Cup
Fresh tomatoes by choice
Chicken pieces 300g

Method:

  1. Heat the tortillas until air pockets form.
  2. Add the cheese and other ingredients.
  3. Lower the heat and cover pan.
  4. Fold the tortilla over.
  5. Remove quesadilla from pan and cut into wedges.
  6. Serve with salad or toppings by choice.

Simple Steak

  • PREP TIME - 35 mins
  • COOK TIME - ~10 mins
  • TOTAL TIME - ~45 mins
  • SERVING - 1 Steak...

Ingredients:

INGREDIENT AMOUNT
Steak 1
Salt By feel
Pepper By feel
Olive oil 1 tablespoon
Garlic 1 or 2 cloves
Fresh herbs Few pinches

Method:

  1. Remove the steak from the fridge and let it come to room temperature for about 30 minutes.
  2. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper on both sides, then add your cut garlic and herbs on top.
  3. Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add the olive oil.
  4. Once the oil is hot, add the steak to the pan. Cook for 4-5 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for an additional 4-5 minutes on the other side.
  5. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
