DESSERTS

Ice Cream

  • PREP TIME - 20 mins
  • FREEZE TIME - 4-12 hours
  • TOTAL TIME - ~5-12 hours
  • SERVING - ~1 liter

Ingredients:

INGREDIENT AMOUNT
Heavy cream 2 Cups
Whole milk 1 Cup
Sugar 2/3 Cup
Sea salt 1/8 teaspoon
Large egg yolks 6
+Topping of choice By choice

Method:

  1. In a small pot, simmer heavy cream, milk, sugar and salt until sugar completely dissolves, about 5 minutes.
  2. Remove pot from heat. In a separate bowl, whisk yolks. Whisking constantly, slowly whisk about a third of the hot cream into the yolks, then whisk the yolk mixture back into the pot with the cream.
  3. Return pot to medium-low heat and gently cook until mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon.
  4. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Cool mixture to room temperature. Cover and chill at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Churn in an ice cream machine according to manufacturers’ instructions. Serve directly from the machine for soft serve, or store in freezer until needed.

Gummy bears

  • PREP TIME - 3 mins
  • COOK TIME - varies
  • FREEZE TIME - ~1 hour
  • TOTAL TIME - ~2-3 hours
  • SERVING - yummy gummy

Ingredients:

INGREDIENT AMOUNT
Fruit juice 1/2 Cup
Unflavored gelatin 3 tablespoons
Honey or Maple syrup 2 tablespoons

Method:

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the fruit juice, unflavored gelatin, and honey or maple syrup.
  2. Whisk the ingredients together over low heat until the gelatin is completely dissolved.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let the mixture cool for a few minutes.
  4. Pour the mixture into gummy bear molds and refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes to an hour.
  5. Once set, gently remove the gummy bears from the molds and store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
