DRINKS

Margarita Cocktail

Ingredients:

INGREDIENT AMOUNT
Tequila 60ml
Lime juice 30ml
Simple syrup 30ml
Salt Small pile
Lime By choice
Ice By choice

Method:

  1. Add tequila, lime juice, syrup and ice. Shake violently for ~30 seconds.
  2. Serve in glass with salt and lime on the side.
