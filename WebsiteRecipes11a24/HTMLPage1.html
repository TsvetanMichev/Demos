Pulled Pork Doughnut Hole Sliders

  • PREP TIME - 10 mins
  • COOK TIME - ~10 hours
  • TOTAL TIME - ~10 hours
  • SERVING - 1 large tray

Ingredients:

INGREDIENT AMOUNT
Root Beer 2 Liters
Barbecue Sauce 1+1/2 Cups
Salt 1+1/2 Cups
Minced Fresh Gingerroot 1 Teaspoon
Pork Shoulder Roast ~3 Pounds

Method:

  1. In a large saucepan, bring root beer to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-high; cook, uncovered, until liquid is reduced by half, 30-45 minutes.
  2. Transfer to a 5- or 6-liter. slow cooker. Stir in barbecue sauce, salt and ginger. Add roast, turning to coat.
  3. Cook, covered, on low until pork is tender, 8-10 hours. For slaw, in a large bowl, mix mayonnaise, vinegar and syrup. Stir in coleslaw mix. Refrigerate, covered, until flavors are blended, at least 1 hour.
  4. Remove pork from slow cooker, skim fat from cooking juices. Remove meat from bones, shred with 2 forks. Return juices and pork to slow cooker, heat through.
  5. To serve, cut doughnut holes in half, cut a thin slice off bottoms to level. Serve pork and slaw in doughnut holes, secure with skewers. If desired, serve with additional barbecue sauce.

Buffalo Chicken Dip

  • PREP TIME - 5 mins
  • COOK TIME - ~25 mins
  • TOTAL TIME - ~30 mins
  • SERVING - 1 small tray

Ingredients:

INGREDIENT AMOUNT
Cream cheese 200g
Cooked chicken breast 1 Cup
Buffalo wing sauce 1/2 Cup
Ranch or blue cheese salad dressing 1/2 cup
Jack cheese 2 cups

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Spread cream cheese into an ungreased shallow 1-liter baking dish.
  3. Layer with chicken, wing sauce and salad dressing. Sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Bake, uncovered, until cheese is melted, 20-25 minutes.
  5. Serve with baguette slices.
